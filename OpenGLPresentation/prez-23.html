Theory: Shaders

How does the GPU actually translate those vertices and indices you stored as arrays into pixels on the screen?

Well it's complicated... and sadly it's 90% your responsibility.

It begins by making programs called Shaders. In OpenGL there are two required kinds of shaders, Vertex and Fragment.

Vertex Shaders are ran on each vertex you feed in.
Fragment Shaders are ran for each pixel in the glViewport.
