Theory: Entry Points

Often when interacting when interacting with any API, it's helpful to think in terms of entry points. OpenGL has a preferred workflow (often called a pipeline).

At various entry points in the pipeline, not only do you prepare items in it context, you also write programs called Shaders that tells the GPU where to draw each vertex on the screen and how to rasterize the colors of the surfaces described.

To start tying things together, lets start drawing.
